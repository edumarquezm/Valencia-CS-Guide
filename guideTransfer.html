TRANSFER OUT OF VALENCIA COLLEGE

This is the end of the guide, and hopefully the end of your time at Valencia College.

I HIGHLY RECOMMEND starting the transfer process 2 semesters before the desired start date at other university. For example: if you want to start at UCF on Fall 2025, I recommend starting the transfer process on Spring 2025. There is a lot of info that needs to be sent and a lot of back-and-forth emails with their admission team, so make sure to start the process early.

When you are accepted as a transfer student in UCF, most of your classes will show as "incomplete" because they have to be reviewed by their admissions team, and it might take a while. In my experience, it took a few months for all my classes to be "verified", but I was still allowed to register for classes through a specific "override" system that they offer, so do not worry if your classes have not been processed yet.

To check all the classes you have taken at Valencia College, make sure to check your "Unofficial Transcript" located in your Atlas account.
